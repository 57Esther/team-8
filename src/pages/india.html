Let's Celebrate With India!

Did you know?

In Bombay, now known as Mumbai, a tradition thrived where communities would construct an effigy representing the 'old man' symbolizing the passing year. At midnight, this effigy was set ablaze, signifying the departure of past grievances and the welcoming of a new year.
The significance extended beyond mere symbolism; it fostered a communal celebration uniting people across diverse backgrounds. Bombay's cosmopolitan nature, home to individuals of varied faiths, allowed for a rich tapestry of festivals. However, the tradition of burning the 'old man' stood out as an inclusive ceremony that transcended age and religious beliefs.

3 Best Places in India for New Year’s Eve

1. Gulmarg

It said about Kashmir, “Gar fidous bar Ruye Zami Ast/Hami Asto Hami Asto Hami Ast” in English translation (if there is heaven on earth, it is here, it is here, it is here). Gulmarg is a part of this haven (Kashmir), for those who want to greet new year eve 2024 in the lap of nature must visit this city and experience something unusual. This is one the best places in India to celebrate new year eve 2024 with your family and friends, where kids can build a snowman with ice, and adults can have new year’s party and fun activities. Be mindful this place can only be your best place for the new year celebration if you like snow and peace.

2. Goa

Isn’t it great to have a beach party this new year eve 2024? If this sounds appealing, then welcome to Goa for the new year eve celebration. With golden-sand 100 beaches, tangled palm trees touching the sky, and a relaxed atmosphere, Goa stands in stark contrast from the other bustling location. It might sound as common as many other places in India. But This place definitely beats any Indian beaches cities when it comes to making the best of any festive memorable. A wacky New Year’s Eve party in Goa, , is one of the best escapes you can plan to ring in New Year with your fun-loving friends. Even though the metros continue to raise their bar all night of crazy celebration, the Goa version is still the most crazed of all. With fireworks and zest all along the coast of Indian’s best state, definitely, it is the best place for the New Year’s Eve party in Goa.

3. Manali

The “Valley of the Gods” is crowded with millions of tourists every year. But on the festivity like New Year’s Eve, it is worth visiting the place. This gorgeous town is not lesser than Europe, as most people like to celebrate New Year’s Eve in Europe, the fun of enjoying a bonfire along with its chilly atmosphere just double the joy of new year’s merriment.
So Let the new year party begin with friends, family, and loved ones at the one best places in Manali. For the New Year’s Eve party, Old Manali would be Great. Old Manali has a great coffee, so if your idea of the New Year’s Eve celebration is to go with some delectable delicacies, must visit the old Manali eateries. Enjoy the best hippy culture at the time of New Year’s Eve in Manali.
